# Privacy Policy for FIB │Management

Last Updated: June 10, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how FIB │Management ("the Bot") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when used within Discord servers.

The Bot is operated by Benedict Berg as a private individual.

## 2. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect and process the following information:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server (Guild) IDs
* Discord Role IDs
* Message content submitted to the Bot
* System and moderation logs
* Database records required for Bot functionality

## 3. How Information Is Used

The collected information is used exclusively for:

* Providing the Bot's functionality
* Managing Discord server operations
* Storing configuration settings
* Troubleshooting and debugging
* Maintaining service security and stability

## 4. Data Storage

The Bot stores certain information persistently, including:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server IDs
* Discord Role IDs

Message content and logs may be stored temporarily or permanently where necessary for Bot functionality, administration, moderation, or troubleshooting.

## 5. Data Sharing

The operator does not sell, rent, or share collected information with third parties except:

* When required by applicable law;
* When necessary to protect the security or integrity of the Bot and its services.

## 6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect stored information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

## 7. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to operate and maintain the Bot and its associated services.

## 8. User Rights

Users may request information regarding their stored data or request deletion of data where technically feasible and legally permissible.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is intended for users who comply with Discord's Terms of Service and minimum age requirements.
